About Me

My short story

My name is Anton Talalaev and I am a Software Developer. I live in Canada and work in EvoTrux, the company that provides services for shippers and carriers throughout Canada and the US. I became a Software Developer not a long time ago, but my career path started 10 years ago. I assume that my story is quite interesting and a bit inspirational for other people. Let me share it with you.

I started my career path as a legal specialist in a big power generation company in Russia. Overall, I have 8 years of successful work experience in the Law field and I even managed to become a Head of the Legal Department in the biggest Clinical Institute of the Moscow region. I was responsible for such business processes as consulting, analysing, finance, tenders, and administering business processes.

During the last 5 years, I began to show interest in programming. I always loved data analysis and building financial models. During my legal career, I became quite good at using Excel and Visual Basic for Applications (VBA). Once, I started to learn programming with the C language but just for fun. I never thought to change my career because there were lots of benefits starting from the salary range and ending with really good medical insurance. For a couple of years, I felt bad about my job and the responsibilities that I executed. At this period, I many times caught myself thinking I would love to create the programs to describe the whole business processes, to automate casual tasks, to manage the processes from outside, not from inside.

One day my wife told me that I had to quit and to enrol in a brand-new coding school that was just opened in Moscow. She said that she will give me one year to become a Software Developer. During this year she promised to work and to care for our daughter on her own. It was a hard decision for me to do so but I never regret it. Soon I quit and enrolled in a peer-based coding school "42 Network". After the 6 months of my studies, I found the first job in the IT field in the largest bank of Russia. I successfully worked there in an agile team for 7 months. In January 2020 my family and I moved to Canada and started a new life from the very beginning. Here I soon become a Software Developer again and would love to share with you my experience in future posts.
